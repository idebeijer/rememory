Create Bundles

Keep your important files within reach — even if you can't reach them yourself.

Add your files, choose who to trust, and set how many must agree for recovery. Each person gets a self-contained bundle. Everything runs locally — nothing leaves your browser.

How it works

Creating bundles

  • Add your files and choose who you trust
  • Decide how many people must agree to recover
  • Each person gets a unique bundle
  • Send each bundle however works best

Recovery

  • Open recover.html in any browser
  • Combine enough pieces and the files are unlocked
  • No server, no internet, nothing to install

Security

  • Everything runs in your browser — nothing leaves your device
  • This page works offline — save it with File › Save As
  • Protected with age encryption
  • Open source (Apache-2.0)
